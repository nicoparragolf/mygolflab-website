Nico Parra

Founder & Head Coach, MyGolfLab

Professional golf coach with over a decade of experience working with players of all levels—from beginners to PGA Tour professionals.

Former Director of Andes Golf Academy and current head coach at Smooth Golf Co, based in Santiago, Chile. My coaching philosophy centers on understanding each player's unique physical characteristics and building a swing that works with their body, not against it.

I believe there's no universal "perfect" swing—only the perfect swing for YOU. This individualized approach, inspired by TPI methodology, is the foundation of everything we do at MyGolfLab.
